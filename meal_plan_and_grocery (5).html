¾ cupoat milk

½ tspcinnamon
1 tspolive oil (for pan)
½ cupfresh strawberries, sliced
drizzlehoney or maple syrup (optional)
Steps
1
Blend oats, oat milk, banana, and cinnamon until smooth. Rest 3 min.
2
Heat non-stick pan over medium-low with a little olive oil.
3

Red lentil & sweet potato soup
Serves 130 min
ironanti-inflamgentle GI
Include
Days/week:
1
Person 2 days/week:
1
Ingredients
⅓ cupred lentils, rinsed
½ mediumsweet potato, peeled & cubed
1 clovegarlic, minced
¼ tspground turmeric
¼ tspground cumin
1½ cupslow-sodium vegetable broth
1 tbspolive oil
¼lemon, juiced (at serving)
Steps
1
Heat olive oil in a medium pot over medium heat. Add garlic and cook 1 min until fragrant.
2
Add turmeric and cumin. Stir 30 seconds until spices bloom.
3
Add rinsed lentils, sweet potato cubes, and broth. Stir to combine.
4
Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Simmer uncovered 20–25 min until lentils and sweet potato are very soft.
5
Partially or fully blend with an immersion blender to desired texture.
6
Ladle into bowl. Squeeze lemon juice over just before eating.
Batch cooking tip
Make a full pot on Sunday and refrigerate up to 4 days, or freeze individual portions for up to 3 months.

irongentle GIthyroid

Salmon & sweet potato mash
Serves 1–225 min
thyroidanti-inflamgentle GI

Person 2 days/week:
1
Ingredients
1 filletsalmon (~150g)
1 largesweet potato, peeled & cubed
2carrots, sliced
1 tbspolive oil
½lemon, juiced
fresh parsleyto garnish
Steps
1
Boil sweet potato cubes in lightly salted water 12–15 min until fork-tender. Drain and mash with ½ tbsp olive oil.
2
Steam sliced carrots 8 min until soft. Set aside.
3
Pat salmon dry. Season with salt, pepper, and a squeeze of lemon.

Thyroid support

Beef & bell pepper stir-fry over rice
Serves 1–220 min
ironvitamin C

Person 2 days/week:
1
Ingredients
150gbeef cubes
1red or orange bell pepper, sliced
1 cupbok choy, roughly chopped
1 cupcooked white rice
1 tspfresh ginger, grated
1 tbsplow-sodium soy sauce or tamari
1 tspsesame oil
½ tbspolive oil
Steps
1
Cut beef into 2cm cubes. Pat dry.
2
Heat olive oil in a wok or wide pan over high heat until very hot.
3
Add beef in a single layer. Sear 1–2 min without stirring, then toss. Cook 1 min more. Remove and set aside.

Serve immediately over white rice.
Iron pairing
Bell pepper is one of the highest vitamin C vegetables — eating it alongside beef heme iron helps absorb non-heme iron from the bok choy too.
